Baptism

2 Nephi 31:4-13
Jesus Christ was baptized to fufill all righteousness. We should be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ after repenting of our sins.

Mosiah 18:8-17
The covenant of baptism is discussed. We are to take upon ourselves the name of Christ and help all those that we come across.

Mosiah 21:35
Baptism is a testimony to God that we are willing to follow his Son, Jesus Christ.

Alma 7:14-16
After repenting we can be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ to wash away our sins. We can be born again.

3 Nephi 7:24-26
Being baptized is a witness unto God and others that you have repented and received a remission of your sins.

3 Nephi 11-18-41
Jesus Christ teaches the correct manner of baptise. We must all repent and be baptized in His name.

3 Nephi 12:2
Blessed are those who humble themselves and are baptized in the name of Jesus Christ.

Moroni 8:25
Baptism is a fruit of repentance.
