Faith

1 Nephi 3:7
In this verse we learn about the faith that Nephi, a prophet of the Lord, had and how we too can approach situations that are hard, with faith in Jesus Christ.

1 Nephi 7:12
Nephi teaches his brothers that the Lord can do all things for us if we have faith in him.

2 Nephi 9:23
In order to return to our Heavenly Father, we must first have faith in His son Jesus Christ.

2 Nephi 26:13
The Lord makes himself known and works with the children of men according to their faith in Him.

Enos 1:3-8
God does hear and answer our prayers. Through true faith in Jesus Christ and repentance we can be be forgiven of our sins.

Mosiah 3:12
Salvation comes through faith and repentance.

Mosiah 5:12
Through faith in Jesus Christ we can change our hearts and become his children.

Mosiah 27:14
Our prayers are answered according to our faith in Jesus Christ.

Alma 14:26
Alma and Amulek receive strength according to their faith in Jesus Christ and break free from their bonds.

Alma 22:16
Through our faith in Jesus Christ we can receive hope for things that are to come.

Alma 32:17-43
Faith is like a seed where if we plant it and give it nourishment it will grow. Faith is not a perfect knowledge.

Alma 33:23
We should plant the word of God, or Jesus Christ, in our hearts and nourish it with our faith.

Alma 57:25-27
Miracles happen through faith in Jesus Christ. An army of soldiers all survive because of their faith.

Helaman 8:15
Jesus Christ is likened unto the brazen serpent. If we look to Jesus Christ with faith and a desire to follow him we too can be saved.

3 Nephi 17:8
Through faith in Jesus Christ we can be healed, both physically and spiritually.

Ether 12
Through faith in Jesus Christ miracles have taken place throughout all of history. The brother of Jared was able to see Jesus Christ because of his faith.

Moroni 10:26,33,38-44
Through faith in Jesus Christ we can be saved and receive anything which is good. We can do all things through faith in Jesus Christ. In order to have faith we must first have hope.

Moroni 10:4
Faith in Jesus Christ is a necessary part in receiving an answer that the Book of Mormon is true.
