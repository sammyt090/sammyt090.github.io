Repentance

Mosiah 5:2
Repentance can cause a mighty change of heart. It can change our thoughts and desires.

Mosiah 26:29
Mode of repentance is established in the church. If we confess our sins to Jesus Christ and repent we can receive forgiveness.

Alma 14:1
Faith and belief are necessary before we can repent.

Alma 34:33
We should not procrastinate our repentance. A day will come when it will be too late to repent.

Alma 36
The repentance process and experience of Alma the younger. Through faith in Jesus Christ we can repent and be free from our sins.

Alma 42:16,29
If there were no sin or punishment repentance would not be possible. We should only let our sins trouble us into repentance.

3 Nephi 9:20,22
We should no longer practice the shedding of blood but instead offer a contrite spirit and a broken heart. Whoever does repent will and comes unto Christ will be saved.

3 Nephi 27:20
We should repent in order to be baptized and receive the Holy Ghost. By doing so we can be spotless before Christ.

Moroni 6:8
Church members should be forgiven as they repent and seek forgiveness.

Moroni 8:25
The first fruit of repentance is baptism.
