Endure to the End

1 Nephi 3:7
In this verse we learn about the faith that Nephi, a prophet of the Lord, had and how we too can approach situations that are hard, with faith in Jesus Christ.

1 Nephi 13:37
Those who have the gift and power of the Holy Ghost and endure to the end will be lifted up at the last day.

2 Nephi 31:19-20
Once we have entered into the gate and received the Holy Ghost we are still not done. We must continue to have faith in Jesus Christ and feast upon his words.

2 Nephi 32:1-5
After we have entered in the way the Holy Ghost will guide us in enduring to the end.

3 Nephi 15:9
Those who endure to the end will receive eternal life.

3 Nephi 27:16-17
If we endure to the end we will held up by Christ guitless before the Father. Those that do not endure to the end will be cast into the fire and not return.
